Welcome to Rebar Vista

This application allows you to view real-time camera feed from your Raspberry Pi 5, measure distances using the ultrasonic sensor, capture images, and download them to your device. The app works best in fullscreen mode for optimal viewing experience.

How to Use This Application

  • Camera View: The main screen displays a live feed from the Pi 5 camera.
  • Distance Reading: The current distance measured by the ultrasonic sensor is shown at the top of the screen.
  • Capture Images: Click the "Capture Image" button to take a snapshot of the current view.
  • Fullscreen Mode: For the best experience, click the fullscreen icon in the controls.
  • Image Gallery: All captured images are stored in the gallery section at the bottom of the page.
  • Add Descriptions: You can add notes or descriptions to your captured images for reference.
  • Download: Each image can be downloaded individually using the download button.

Tip: For optimal performance, keep the camera and sensor steady and ensure good lighting conditions.
